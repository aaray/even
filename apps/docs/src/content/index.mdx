## About EVEN

EVEN is a direct-to-fan platform for music artists. Artists can sell:

- **Music** - Albums, singles, and exclusive tracks
- **Videos** - Music videos, behind-the-scenes, and live performances
- **Merch** - Physical merchandise and collectibles
- **Experiences** - Meet-and-greets, virtual events, and exclusive access
