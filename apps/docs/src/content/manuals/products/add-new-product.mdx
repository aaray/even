# Adding a New Product

Learn how to create and publish products for your fans on EVEN.

**Estimated time**: 10 minutes
**Difficulty**: Beginner

## Prerequisites

- Artist account with publishing permissions
- Product content ready (files, images, descriptions)

## Product Types

EVEN supports four product categories:

| Category | Examples | File Types |
|----------|----------|------------|
| **Music** | Albums, singles, EPs | MP3, WAV, FLAC |
| **Video** | Music videos, behind-the-scenes | MP4, MOV |
| **Merch** | T-shirts, posters, vinyl | Physical goods |
| **Experience** | Meet-and-greets, virtual events | Tickets/access |

## After Publishing

Once published:
- Product appears in your Products grid
- Fans can find it in your store
- Sales begin tracking automatically
- You'll receive notifications for purchases

## Editing Products

To edit an existing product:
1. Find the product in your Products grid
2. Click the **Edit** button (pencil icon)
3. Make your changes
4. Click **Save** to update

## Common Issues

### Upload Failed

If your upload fails:
- Check your internet connection
- Verify file size is under the limit (500MB for video, 100MB for audio)
- Try a different browser
- Contact support if issues persist

### Product Not Showing

If your product doesn't appear:
- Refresh the page
- Check the filter settings
- Verify the product status is "Published"
